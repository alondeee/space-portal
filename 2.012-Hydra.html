    • Hydra - Foundational Data

  • Rank: largest Constellation
  • Area: 1303 square degrees, 3.16% of the total sky
  • Best Visible: March-May
  • Brightest Star: Alphard - 47th brightest in night sky
  • Mythology: Represents the Lernaean Hydra, the multi-headed water serpent slain by Hercules as his second labor.
  • Nickname: "The Water Snake"
  • Location: Southern celestial hemisphere
  • Neighbours: Cancer, Canis Minor, Centaurus, Corvus, Crater, Leo
