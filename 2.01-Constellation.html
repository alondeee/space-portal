Constellations

A constellation is a recognized area of the sky with defined boundaries, containing a pattern of stars that traditionally represents an animal, mythological character, or object. The International Astronomical Union (IAU) recognizes 88 official constellations covering the entire celestial sphere.

88 Constellations

There are altogether 88 recognized constellation by the IAU (International Astronomical Union)

    The Zodiac (12): Aries, Taurus, Gemini, Cancer, Leo, Virgo, Libra, Scorpius, Sagittarius, Capricornus, Aquarius, Pisces

    Classical Greek (36): Andromeda, Aquila, Ara, Argo Navis*, Auriga, Boötes, Canis Major, Canis Minor, Cassiopeia, Centaurus, Cepheus, Cetus, Corona Australis, Corona Borealis, Corvus, Crater, Cygnus, Delphinus, Draco, Equuleus, Eridanus, Hercules, Hydra, Lepus, Lupus, Lyra, Ophiuchus, Orion, Pegasus, Perseus, Piscis Austrinus, Sagitta, Serpens, Triangulum, Ursa Major, Ursa Minor *Argo Navis was later split into Carina, Puppis, Vela

    Early Modern Northern (9): Camelopardalis, Lacerta, Leo Minor, Columba Lynx, Vulpecula Canes Venatici, Coma Berenices, Scutum, Sextans, Monoceros

    Southern Constellations - "Bayer's Exotics" (12): Apus, Grus, Pavo, Phoenix, Tucana, Chamaeleon, Dorado, Hydrus, Indus, Musca, Volans, Triangulum Australe

    Southern Constellations - "La Caille's Laboratory" (13): Antlia, Caelum, Circinus, Fornax, Horologium, Microscopium, Norma, Octans, Pictor, Reticulum, Telescopium, Sculptor, Mensa

    Split from Argo Navis (4): Carina (The Keel), Puppis (The Stern), Vela (The Sails), Pyxis (The Compass)

  • Libra, Aqiula, Orion
  • Pisces, Serpens, Ophiuchus

Orion's Belt (especially Mintaka/δ Orionis) is almost exactly on the celestial equator.

Zodiacs

12 official zodiac signs, but Ophiuchus is recognized in zodiac signs

    These are a few

  • Aries (The Ram) -- March 21 - April 19 (Sun: Apr 18-May 13)
  • Taurus (the Bull) -- April 20 - May 20 (Sun: May 13-Jun 21)
  • Gemini (The Twins) -- May 21 - June 20 (Sun: Jun 21-Jul 20)
  • Cancer (The Crab) -- June 21 - July 22 (Sun: Jul 20-Aug 10)
  • Leo (The Lion) -- July 23 - August 22 (Sun: Aug 10-Sep 16)
  • Virgo (The Virgin) -- August 23 - September 22 (Sun: Sep 16-Oct 30)
  • Libra (The Scales) -- September 23 - October 22 (Sun: Oct 30-Nov 23)
  • Scorpius (The Scorpion) -- October 23 - November 21 (Sun: Nov 23-29)
  • Sagittarius (The Archer) -- November 22 - December 21 (Sun: Dec 18-Jan 18)
  • Capricornus (The Sea Goat) -- December 22 - January 19 (Sun: Jan 18-Feb 16)
  • Aquarius (The Water Bearer) -- January 20 - February 18 (Sun: Feb 16-Mar 11)
  • Pisces (The Fishes) -- February 19 - March 20 (Sun: Mar 11-Apr 18)
  • Ophiuchus -- Nov 29 - Dec 18 (not recognized by the modern Western astrology)

    • Note: Dates in parentheses show when the Sun is actually in each constellation (astronomical dates). First dates are astrological/tropical zodiac dates.

Facts

  • Brightest Star: Sirius
  • Nearest stars to earth: Proxima Centauri, Alpha Centauri A & B
